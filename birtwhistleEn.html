Birtwhistle

Situation

For this project, I wanted to design a sleek one-pager for Chris Birtwistle, a young furniture maker who creates handmade, modern furniture inspired by Japanese woodworking. The website had to visually convey his craft and minimalist style, while also allowing orders to be placed in person.

Process

The design process focused on a clear layout, modern typography, and consistent branding. To professionally present the furniture (Kumo Table, Hikari Bench, and Mizu Cabinet), I used AI-generated images as product visuals and hero images. An inspiring workspace environment was added to the "About Us" section. Everything is combined into a modern, visually appealing one-pager that reinforces the brand's identity.

Result

The result is a professional-looking website that stylishly presents Chris's furniture. Visitors immediately get a good impression of his designs and can place orders personally via the contact form. The combination of sleek design, AI-generated images, and clear typography creates a compelling pitch for a potential furniture maker website.
