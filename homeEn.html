About Me

I am Marc. I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Communication and Multimedia Design from the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. I also studied Graphic Design at the Amsterdam Regional Training Center (ROC van Amsterdam).

I design visual products that meet the user's needs. Through research, testing, and iteration, I consistently strive for the best possible result.

Creativity and a thirst for knowledge drive me: I love to keep discovering new techniques and ideas to improve my work.
