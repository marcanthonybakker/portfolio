Spurd Instagram Posts

Situation

For my graphic design portfolio, I wanted to practice creating social media posts. I chose Spurd, a sports initiative in Purmerend, as my inspiration to design content aimed at an active and approachable audience.

Process

To guide the design, I used a social media strategy developed with ChatGPT. Based on this strategy, I created AI image prompts to generate fitting sports-related visuals. I then edited the images in Figma, carefully removing the backgrounds and designing the layouts for the posts.

Result

The final outcome is a series of fresh, visually engaging social media posts that reflect Spurdâ€™s energy and spirit. This practice project demonstrates my ability to combine strategy, AI tools, and design software to create a professional-looking result.
