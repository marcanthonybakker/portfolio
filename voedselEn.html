Situation

For Siyou, I was tasked with creating a pitch design for Voedsel Verbindt, an initiative that promotes local and sustainable food across different regions of North Holland. The platform needed to allow users to log in and apply filters such as region and content type.

Process

Within a short and focused timeline, I designed the pitch layout and developed a clear user flow that included the required filtering options. I also had the opportunity to create a new logo, which reflects the projectâ€™s sustainable and local character.
