Sailor Skelly

Situation

For this project, I wanted to explore how a playful user experience can be enhanced through animation and character design. This was my first time working with Adobe After Effects, and I learned a lot from it.

Process

I designed a cartoonish illustration style, inspired by the 1950s, and combined this with an animated character that the user can control with simple buttons. Despite the limited compatibility of Adobe XD, the project offered room to experiment with the basic principles of After Effects.

Result

The final product was an interactive UI with a unique, playful look and provided me with valuable experience in animation and motion design.
