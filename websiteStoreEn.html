R2B | Website productpage redesign

Situation

For the R2B Store, I worked on the redesign of the website. So far, I have created designs for the product search page and the product detail page. The goal was to improve the user experience and create a cleaner, more structured interface.

Process

When designing the product detail page, I focused on keeping the product visible at all times. By using dropdowns, menus, and separate screens, I was able to distribute options more clearly and prevent users from having to make too many decisions at once.

Result

The design resulted in a clickable prototype, supported by clear annotations for the developers. Step by step, the entire website is being redesigned, with these pages serving as the foundation for further development and optimization.
