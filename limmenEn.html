Banketbakkerij Limmen

Situation

For this project, I set out to redesign an existing bakery website to create a fresh and user-friendly experience. The original site contained too many separate pages and repetitive content, making navigation confusing for visitors.

Process

I began with AI-driven research into the best bakery websites and translated the findings into a mood board. From there, I created a wireframe and refined the design with additional AI feedback on style and structure. Along the way, I merged related pages, reduced duplicate information, and introduced smart filter options for each category page.

Result

The final design is a clean, modern website with generous white space and intuitive navigation. Visitors can now quickly find their favorite products, and the bakery can easily showcase new content and promotions.
