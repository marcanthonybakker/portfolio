Purmerends Museum Redesign

Situation

Previously, I worked on a project for the Purmerends Museum called Portret in Perspectief. In that project, I was able to experiment with a new visual style for the brand. This inspired me to redesign several website screens in a similar direction.

Process

While revisiting the earlier project, I decided not to use the exact same style. Instead, I made new design choices that I felt better matched the museumâ€™s current identity. For example, I introduced more white space, used larger and more prominent imagery, and adjusted the placement of visuals to give the content more breathing room.

Result

The final design differs from Portret in Perspectief but feels fresher and more balanced. These adjustments create a clean, spacious look that represents the museum more effectively. Looking back, I even feel that I would restyle the previous project to bring it up to the same new standard.
